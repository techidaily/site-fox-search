## Detect Installed Microsoft Office App Dialog

From this dialog you can add a condition to detect the Microsoft Office App version already installed on the target machine. A search will be performed at the beginning of the installation and the condition will be true only if the Office App installed on the target machine has a**higher or equal** version than the one selected in this dialog.

![Detect Installed Office App Dialog](https://cdn.advancedinstaller.com/img/dialog/detect-installed-office.png "Detect Installed Office App Dialog")  

From the left combo box you can select the Microsoft Office App and from the right combo box the year.

![Note](https://cdn.advancedinstaller.com/svg/common/IconMessageNote.svg)If there are several versions of the Office App installed on the target machine, the version selected in this dialog will be compared with the highest version from the target machine.
